Ask & Answer Questions

Students can post a question for support, answer others’ questions and track the most heated question.

Students can revise and ​customize ​each project phase throughout the project. The instructor can ​confirm the progress​ and also ​add new goals for students during check-ins.

Save Bookmarks and Resources

Both instructor and students can upload useful project resources in the shared resource box. Students can also bookmark useful reply for future reference.

Mark Breakthroughs

To help students document their triumphs, breakthroughs are designed to help students reflect on their solutions when they encounter similar processes in the future.
